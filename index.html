Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?

+

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use?

+

Yes, this website is completely free to use. You can access lessons, vocabulary practice, and exercises without any cost.

3. Do I need to create an account?

+

No, you do not need to create an account to start learning. However, creating an account can help you track your progress and save your learning history.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary?

+

You can build your vocabulary by learning new words daily, practicing with exercises, reading examples, and using the words in sentences.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

+

Currently, we do not offer certificates. Our main goal is to help you improve your English skills through practical learning and regular practice.
